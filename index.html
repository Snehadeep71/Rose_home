Welcome to

The Rose

The Rose Kitchen is a delightful fusion of a confectionery and a multicuisine restaurant, proudly serving Kolkata since 1996. With four vibrant outlets spread across the city, we offer an inviting atmosphere where culinary excellence meets warm hospitality. Our menu boasts a diverse range of delectable dishes, from traditional Indian classics to innovative contemporary creations, ensuring a memorable dining experience for every guest. At The Rose Kitchen, we are committed to quality, authenticity, and the joy of sharing great food with great company. Join us to indulge in flavors that tell a story of passion and tradition.
